Set a constant scaling to a band covering the given frequency bins (inclusively) for the given channels. Frequency bin \char`\"{}i\char`\"{} is from i$\ast$f to (i+1)$\ast$f where f is 1.\+0/(nsamples$\ast$tick). The largest meaningful frequency bin is nsamples/2. The frequency band is {\itshape inclusive} of both min and max frequency bins. Note, it\textquotesingle{}s up to caller to appropriately segment multiple masks across multiple channels. For any given channel, last call to this method wins.
